Country Festival

Join us for the best Country Festival!

Festival HighLights

  • Best Dressed Contest: Show off your country hat and boots!
  • Live Country Music: Traditional tunes to keep you dancing.
  • Rustic Decor: Colorful flags and straw ornaments.
  • The Massive Bonfire: The heart of our celebration!

Attractions & Games

  • Barn Dance: A fun group dance for everyone.
  • Fishing Game: Catch a fish and win a prize!
  • Ring Toss: Test your aim and skills.
  • Photo Booth: Capture your memories with western props.
  • Mechanical Bull: Test your courage and see how long you can ride!

Food & Drinks

  • Corn on the Cob: Served hot with melted butter.
  • Creamy Corn Pudding: A traditional sweet treat (Canjica).
  • BBQ Skewers: Grilled meat sticks, Brazilian style.
  • Cornbread & Cakes: Moist and delicious artisan cakes.
  • Hot Mulled Wine: Our special spicy winter drink.
