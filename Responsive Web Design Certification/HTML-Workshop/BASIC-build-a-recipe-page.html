International Shrimp

This recipe is for you if you love Coco Bambu's international shrimp dish. See how to make an identical international shrimp recipe! You'll be amazed by the result!

Ingredients

  • Cleaned shrimp (800g)
  • Lemon 1
  • Chopped ham (200g)
  • Chopped mozzarella cheese (200g)
  • Sliced mozzarella cheese (100g)
  • Peas (1 can)
  • Rice Cooked (approximately 4 cups)
  • Cream (2 cartons)
  • Cream cheese (200 ml)
  • Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Potato sticks
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)

      • Instructions

      1. Season the cleaned shrimp with salt, lime juice, and pepper. Let it marinate for at least 15 minutes.
      2. Melt the butter in a pan and then add the shrimp.
      3. Saut√© the shrimp. Be careful, as shrimp cooks very fast and becomes rubbery if overcooked; it is ready as soon as it turns pink.
      4. Add the peas, heavy cream, cream cheese, ham, and cheese. Once everything is mixed, add the rice.
      5. Transfer the mixture to a baking dish, cover with sliced mozzarella, and bake for about 15 minutes.
      6. Remove from the oven, top with shoestring potatoes, and it is ready to serve.
